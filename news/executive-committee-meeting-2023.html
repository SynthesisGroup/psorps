Executive Committee Meeting 2023

January 30, 2023

New year 2023, full of optimism and vigor the executive committee of 2023 – 2024 assembled for the first time last January 19 at Manduca Taberna, BGC. After having spent most of the previous meetings remotely, our team of officers headed by President-elect Dr. Yvette Santiago, have outlined several activities for the society, with more face-to-face meetings, medical missions, round table discussions, and collaborations. Among these are the anticipated ASEAN Ophthalmology Society (AOS) and Philippine Academy of Ophthalmology (PAO) meeting later in the year, where PSOPSRS will have a special session together with a panel of international experts in the field. Our surgical mission will be back and the first for this year will serve the Pampanga region. Expect more learning sessions, reunions and other activities ahead!
