PSOPRS Members Speak Globally

October 12, 2020

Zoom! Zoom! Zoom! PSOPRS members join international virtual conferences as lecturers, reactors and contributors, sharing cases, expertise and camaraderie. Together, we join them in unceasing learning.

Dr. Raoul Henson shared a case on “Mucocele Decompression” last June 20, 2020 during the All India Ophthalmologic Society (AIOS) Oculoplasty and Facial Aesthetics Case Files Global Webinar.

Dr. Reynaldo Javate joined the World Ophthalmologic Congress (WOC) last June 28, 2020 as he lectured on the “Surgical technique and Success Rate of Transcanalicular Endoscopic Lacrimal Duct Recanalization (TELDR) With Balloon Dacryoplasty and Silicone Intubation”.

Our Lacrimal experts Dr. Reynaldo Javate and Dr. Raoul Henson chaired a session during the Dacryology webinar by All India OphthalmoloAIOS) last July 26, 2020.

We support also our PSOPRS member Dr. Charisse Tanlapco who was a speaker in the August 19, 2020 Phacomentors Community lecture on “ Learn Phaco Fast & Efficiently”.

Dr. Yvette Santiago-Gatmaitan presented a very interesting case entitled “Creepy Crawlies” during the International Ocular Adnexal Oncology Webinar last August 30, 2020.

And Dr. Rian Parulan discussed a case on “Subgaleal Hematoma with Orbital Extension” during the 5th Asia Pacific Ophthalmic Trauma Society Conference last Sept. 5, 2020

Our APSOPRS and PSOPRS President Dr. Raoul Henson has delivered 2 very-well attended APSOPRS webinars. The 1stAPSOPRS webinar was held last July 18, 2020 discussing “Challenging Oculofacial Cases”. The second talked about “Challenging Eyelids: A Spectrum of Opportunities” last September 26, 2020.
