1st Exec Meeting & Schedule Of Activities For 2021

January 19, 2021

2021 here we come! With renewed hope, new plans, and goals to bring all PSOPRS members together once again, our elected officers for the year 2021-2022 held its first Execom meeting last January 14, via zoom. We have adapted to the new normal of being remote from each other. Headed by our new President-elect Dr. Charmaine Ang, the executive committee has prepared a lot of activities for this year, new participant hospitals for the round table discussions, “mini-meets” for members only, and the creation of sub-committees where the young once meets the young ones. Watch out for these activities!
