PSOPRS IN APSOPRS – DEC 11-12, 2021

News and Events

The 3rd Webinar of APSOPRS-INASOPRS was graced by our very own Dr. Mary Rose Pe-Yan, who was a speaker on Day 1, with a very catchy discussion on phtisis bulbi: “Catching the Enemy with Enemies”. While on the 2nd day, Dr. Reynaldo Javate, who is one of the founding member of APSOPRS, not only moderated the session, but gave his own lecture as well, “Rejuvination of the id-Cheek Interface: Lower Lid Belpharoplasty with Mini Midface Lift”. This was followed by a case presentation by Dr. Yvette Santiago-Gatmaitan, “A Case of Complete Paralytic Ptosis”, and another interesting discussion by our PSOPRS Vice-president, Dr. Katrina Ranche, “ Eyelid Asymmetry After Belpharoplasty”. A panel of experts from both societies livened up each session with engaging discussions.
